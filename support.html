About and How This Works

This video downloader supports about 1000 websites due to it being based on a free utility called youtube-dl. This website is essentially nothing more than a friendly interface for the traditional command line based utility.

Due to the nature of big websites like youtube and instagram, APIs and other download methods used by youtube-dl can be found to be frequntly broken. And since this utility is free and open source, it is maintained by nothing more than volunteers. This means that if a video fails to download (like instagram), it most likely means that the website changed some crucial source of information required by youtube-dl to successfully download the video and it could take several days or months before official support for downloading from the broken website is restored by the youtube-dl maintainers.

Videos are downloaded in the highest quality availible. This means downloads may take some time to process so please be patient!

Broken or Unsupported

Instagram


As of now, Instagram is broken in youtube-dl. However, I was able to make a workaround using gallery-dl.

C-span


Some c-span videos like this one currently do not work. This is because this video was not uploaded by c-span themselves and is missing important information required by youtube-dl to download.
You can tell which c-span videos are broken if they have "This clip, title, and description were not created by C-SPAN." In orange at the top of the video under its title.

Netflix (vandam.netflix or netflix.com)

Netflix does not, and will never be supported. This is copyrighted content and will never be supported by youtube-dl. It is also heavily protected with DRM making downloads very difficult. If you try to download anything with netflix.com in the name, expect this to fail.
